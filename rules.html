RULES

Objective: The goal is to move your frog across a busy road and a river to reach a safe zone at the top of the screen. Avoid obstacles and enemies along the way.

Movement: Use the arrow keys (or designated controls) to move the frog:

  • Up: Move the frog upward.
  • Down: Move the frog downward.
  • Left: Move the frog left.
  • Right: Move the frog right.

    • Obstacles:

  • Cars and Trucks: Move along the road, and you must avoid being hit by them.
  • Water: When you reach the river, you need to use logs, turtles, and other floating objects to cross safely. If you fall into the water, you lose a life.

    • Lives: You have a limited number of lives. Losing a life occurs when:

  • You are hit by a car/truck.
  • You fall into the water or fail to reach a floating object.

    • Safe Zones: At the top of the screen, there are "safe zones." The frog must reach these zones to complete a level. Each zone corresponds to a family of frogs.

    Level Progression: As you complete levels, the game speeds up, and the difficulty increases with more cars, faster-moving objects, and more challenging obstacles.

    Winning: The game ends when you either lose all your lives or complete all the levels. The goal is to get as far as possible without losing all lives.
